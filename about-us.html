ABOUT US

FOXTEL INFRA PRIVATE LIMITED

Foxtel Infra Pvt Ltd was formed in 2018 with the objective of establishing a sustainable and continuingly improving, specializing in construction of IT and Civil Infrastructure. We are recognized for our innovative approach and professionalism in the fields of IT and Civil Industries. Our company brings original and workable solutions to the problems and challenges faced by IT and Civil Industries

One of Foxtel Infra’s greatest strengths is its ability to assume control of an entire project and bring to it an integrated solution. Foxtel Infra applies an integrated approach to projects, including turnkey solutions. We always strive to exceed our clients’ goals and expectations with structural flexibility and extensive feild experience, our company creates value for its clients through made-to-measure solutions.

Surrounded by a talented staff of specialists and providing a full range of services Foxtel Infra is a one-stop centre that responds to the various needs of our clients and also guides clients through the design, implementation, optimization and support of ambitious and complex projects

Recently we have extended our business verticals by adding up the Rice Trading and Metal Trading to our portfolio. With a vision of creating a distribution network across India and Global market.
